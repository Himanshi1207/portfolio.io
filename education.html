SCHOOL

St. Martin's Diocesan School has a rich legacy within the portals of a calm and serene environment, in the heart of the Cantonment area of New Delhi. The personalities of thousands of Martiners have been polished and shaped by this noble institution which began with a vision by its founder Ms. Helen D. Jerwood in 1960.

Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT) is a seat of higher technical education in India. Prof. Jai Prakash Saini is the Founder Vice-Chancellor of Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT), New Delhi.
